A checklist for visualisations

When you have painstakingly made your pictures, it is time to add them to your text. Here are some things to take into account. 

  • Check the size of the picture. Will your reader be able to read the smallest font?
  • Check the picture layout. Is the picture displayed correctly (not mirrored, upside down, rotated or shifted)?
  • Check the page layout. Does your text look good with the picture?
  • Check the caption. Does it stay directly above the table or underneath the figure?
