Guidelines for appendices

Most technical reports will have appendices. By incorporating appendices, the story in the body text will remain clear and concise. All supplementary information can be found in the appendices. Appendices can be more extensive than the body text as long as they are relevant to the body text.

Appendices are part of the back matter. Put them after the references. There the appendices are treated as stand-alone entities.
