A personal touch is allowed

Because the preface is not strictly part of the subject of the report, it is the only place in your report where you are allowed to use a personal tone of voice.

In the preface it is allowed to address the reader directly. You cannot do this in the rest of your report. An example:

“We hope that our report inspires you to do something in the field of Artificial Intelligence. This project definitely inspired us.”

Did you like the project you have been working on? Say so! Social niceties are always appreciated. Teachers are human too.

What if you hated the project? In that case you better keep a neutral voice.
