Deciding on visualisations

If you want to add visualisations, you have three decisions to make.

  1. Which point do you want to make with the visualisation? How does this visualisation prove the point you have made in your text? Which aspect of your design or research do you want to show?
  2. Which kind of visualisation do you want to use? What is the best way to deliver the message? A pie chart or a bar chart? A graph, table or picture?
  3. Which design will you use? How many colours and which? What about size, clarity etc.?

Evaluate

Evaluate the choices that have been made by students. Three pairs of visualisations will be shown. Which one is to be preferred?
