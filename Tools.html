Guidelines for structuring a text

In order to grasp the meaning of a text, a reader is required to put in an effort. When reading, a reader will look for clues that will help him to follow the train of thought of the writer.

As a writer you provide these clues by using sections, paragraphs, headings, lists, numbers, words that are italic or bold, et cetera. Use these clues carefully and consciously. Follow the rules. See what may happen if you choose not to do so…
