How to distinguish paragraphs?

There are two ways to start a new paragraph: you can skip a line (using Enters) or indent (using Tabs). In terms of genre: white lines are often used in technical writing (e.g. reports), indentation is mostly used in academic writing (e.g. academic research articles), but there are exceptions. Make sure that you are consistent throughout your document.

Paragraphs should not cover more than one topic. Therefore, it is important to check: if you notice that a new topic is introduced, you should start a new paragraph.
