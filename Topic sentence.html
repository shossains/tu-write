The first sentence of a paragraph is the topic sentence

Ideally, the topic sentence is the first sentence of a paragraph. This is where most readers expect it to be. A reader with little time will scan a text by reading the first sentences of the paragraphs. If the text has been constructed well, he will get a pretty good idea of the contents, as the topic sentence gives the most important information of a paragraph.

After you have examined an example, you will see that there are two types of topic sentences: announcements and statements.

Two types

There are two types of topic sentences:

  • Type 1: Announcement
    This topic sentence announces what will be discussed in a paragraph. 
  • Type 2: Statement
    This topic sentence is a statement that contains the subject of the paragraph. The remainder of the paragraph supports or clarifies the statement.
