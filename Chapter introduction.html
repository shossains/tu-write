How to write a chapter introduction?

Start each chapter with a short introduction. This provides an overview that your readers will use to decide whether they need to read the chapter, or where they can find the information that they are specifically interested in. 

Contents of the chapter introduction

  • Mention the purpose of the chapter. What is the main topic? Which main question will you answer?
  • Describe the outline. What will you discuss in the consecutive sections?

Format of the chapter introduction

  • Usually  one or two paragraphs will do to introduce the contents of a chapter. Do not treat a short introduction as a section. No section number and section title are needed here.
  • If you need more space (half a page), consider to put the introduction into a section. Add a section number and section title: x.1 Introduction. However, if you do this, then you probably will need to do this in all your chapters.
