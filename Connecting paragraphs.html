Two ways to connect paragraphs

To allow for smooth reading, you can use these two tricks to connect paragraphs.

1. Create a ‘bridge’

If the subject of the topic sentence is an object or subject in the last sentence of the preceding paragraph, you have created a bridge. 

2. Use signposts

Use signposts (signaalwoorden) to show how paragraphs are connected. Your reader will be grateful if you do. There are many signposts that you can use to indicate a connection.
