Guidelines for the summary

The summary is a very important part of your report that is read by almost all readers. Here you will learn about the functions and the guidelines of the summary.

The summary has three functions, which are especially useful for readers with little time. 

  1. The summary defines the subject.
     After having read the summary, your reader knows which subjects and perspectives your report or paper covers. 
  2. The summary gives an overview.
    The summary gives the reader a good idea of the contents of your report or paper.
  3. The summary helps to decide.
    Based on your summary, the reader will decide whether or not to read the rest of your report or paper.
