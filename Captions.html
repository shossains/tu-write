The art of caption writing

Each figure and each table in your report should have a caption. The caption tells what the figure or table shows so that it can be understood independently. In some domains, captions can be quite extensive, see the figure.

Make sure that all captions have a number and have the same format throughout your report or paper. Use the same font and punctuation marks.

It is advised to choose a font for captions that differs from the regular font. Otherwise your reader might think that the story continues in the caption.
