Prepare your reader

A chapter introduction needs to prepare the reader for the chapter. For this, it should:

  • link chapter title and chapter introduction;
  • clearly state the purpose of the chapter;
  • announce the chapter structure, including section numbers.

Evaluate two chapter introductions

The first chapter introduction is derived from a report written for the 3mE Design Contest. The second one stems from a report composed by Computer Science and Engineering students. They had to program an artificial intelligence multi-agent system capable of playing the game StarCraft at an advanced level.
